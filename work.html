Client · Portfolio site

Andrea — Paintings & Ceramics

A clean, responsive portfolio for a painter and ceramic artist. The goal was to highlight the work without the layout getting in the way, and to make it simple for visitors to browse collections and reach out.

  • Role: Layout design, visual direction, site structure.
  • Focus: Simplicity, spacing, easy navigation, mobile-first experience.
  • Outcome: A cohesive home for her work, with a gallery flow that feels calm and intentional.

Concept · Small business

Local Services Brand Concept

Identity and single-page web concept for a local services business. The goal was to build trust quickly: show proof of work, make contact easy, and reduce uncertainty through clear messaging.

  • Role: Logo direction, color system, layout, messaging structure.
  • Focus: “Reliable, local, trustworthy” feeling with clear calls-to-action.
  • Outcome: A flexible visual system that can extend to flyers, uniforms, and vehicles.
